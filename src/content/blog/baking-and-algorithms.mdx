date: "March 15, 2026"
---
In baking, a sourdough starter is a living culture that you must "feed" to keep alive. 
In Computer Science, this reminds me of a **recursive function**. 

Each feeding is a base case: 
1. If the starter is active, bake bread (Base Case).
2. If the starter is hungry, feed it and wait (Recursive Step).

### Why the Precision Matters
Just like a semicolon in C++, 5 grams of extra water can change the entire structure 
of a loaf.
